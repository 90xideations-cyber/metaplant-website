Technical Capability FAQ

Is MetaPlant3D fully compatible with standard CAD formats? expand_more

Yes. MetaPlant supports bi-directional synchronization with DWG, DGN, and IFC formats, ensuring your legacy data remains central to the new ecosystem.

What IoT protocols does the Digital Twin support? expand_more

We natively support MQTT, OPC UA, and Modbus TCP/IP, with custom integration layers for proprietary SCADA systems.

Can the platform handle massive plant models in a browser? expand_more

MetaPlant utilizes proprietary spatial tiling and occlusion culling technology, allowing for smooth 60FPS navigation of plants with over 100,000 components.

How secure is the cloud-native infrastructure? expand_more

We offer AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit, SOC2 Type II compliance, and on-premise private cloud deployment options for highly sensitive industrial sites.

Does MetaPlant offer offline capabilities for field engineers? expand_more

Yes. Field engineers can cache specific asset modules to their tablets for offline inspection, with auto-syncing once connectivity is restored.
